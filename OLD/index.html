Who Are We?

Girls Code Club is an organization hosted by the Lancaster Science Factory that strives to expose girls aged 8-13 to the world of coding, and coding-related concepts. This is a monthly facilitated meetup.

Lessons 2022-2023

For the 2022-2023 year, Cohort 1 will be learning Python while Cohorts 2 & 3 learn JavaScript! All cohorts will be learning the same concepts at the same time, but in different coding languages. Given that Cohort 1 largely consists of returning students, they will also have the opportunity to vote on our next "Hall of Heroes" plaque (dedicated to Girls Code Club)!

This session includes GCC expectations, Internet Safety, usernames, and our "SHEro of the Month" (Radia Joy Perlman a.k.a. "Mother of the Internet") for all cohorts. In addition...

Cohort 1

  • Python Variables
  • Voting on a SHEro to add to the Lancaster Science Factory's "Hall of Heroes"!

Cohorts 2 & 3

  • Typing 101: Stretching our Coding Fingers
