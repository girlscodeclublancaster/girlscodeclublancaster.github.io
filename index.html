COVID-19 Update from LSF

The Lancaster Science Factory takes health and safety measures seriously and continues to enforce the following mask requirement inside the facility: CLOTH MASKS THAT COVER THE NOSE AND MOUTH FOR ALL GUESTS OVER THE AGE OF 2 ARE REQUIRED IN OUR FACILITY. NO EXCEPTIONS. Face or mouth shields alone are NOT acceptable. While State regulations have relaxed for fully vaccinated individuals, we understand that many families that visit the Science Factory will be with children who are not yet fully vaccinated or able to be vaccinated. We remain committed to keeping our facility OPEN and SAFE for everyone to enjoy.
